About

I have been painting for over 40 years. I led teams in my early career before going off on my own. For the last 20 years, I have been in Matamata, and White Dove is well-known as the best you can get.

I am both fast and a perfectionist. You won't be disappointed if you choose me for your next job, whether you have a beautiful new home or a garage to get done.

I have a scaffold, a sprayer, plastering equipment, and a water-blaster on hand, so I can take care of plenty of jobs other painters have to delegate.
